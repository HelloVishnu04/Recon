Detectable Threat Categories

Low Risk

Normal Traffic

Legitimate network communications including standard HTTP, FTP, SSH, and DNS activity.

High Risk

DoS Attack

Denial of Service — SYN floods, Smurf attacks, Teardrop, Neptune, and resource exhaustion.

Medium Risk

Probe Attack

Reconnaissance — Port scanning, IP sweeps, Nmap probes, and Satan vulnerability scans.

High Risk

R2L Attack

Remote to Local — Password guessing, phishing, FTP write access, and unauthorized remote entry.

Critical

U2R Attack

User to Root — Buffer overflow, rootkit installation, Perl exploits, and privilege escalation.
